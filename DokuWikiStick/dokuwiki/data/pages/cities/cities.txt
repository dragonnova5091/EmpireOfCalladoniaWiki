====== Cities of Serberistan ======

**The 7 Grand Cities**

  * [[Paterdoma|Paterdoma]] 
  * [[Vantocar|Vantocar]]
  * [[Surniam|Surniam]]
  * [[Iliamna|Iliamna]]
  * [[Alakan|Alakan]]
  * [[Elsamera|Elsamera]]
  * [[Dragnara|Dragnara]].


Surrounding these 7 cities are smaller villages and towns, many lay empty since the fall of the 3rd age. Some still maintain a permanent population, while others serve as a shelter for the traveling [[Sansuvut Nomads|Sansuvut Nomads]]. 
These cities typically lay a day or two's walk from each other, lending travelers a place to rest safely as they make their pilgrimages across Serberistan.
