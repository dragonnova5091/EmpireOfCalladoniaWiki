====== Cities of Serberistan ======

Across the continent lay 7 major cities. The oldest and Grandest of these are the rival cities of [[Paterdoma|Paterdoma]] and [[Vantocar|Vantocar]]. 
Spread across the nation are 5 smaller cities, [[Surniam|Surniam]], [[Iliamna|Iliamna]], NameTBD, [[Elsamera|Elsamera]], [[Dragnara|Dragnara]].
Surrounding these 7 cities are smaller villages and towns, many lay empty since the fall of the 3rd age. Some still maintain a permanent population,
while others serve as a shelter for the traveling [[Sansuvut Nomads|Sansuvut Nomads]].
